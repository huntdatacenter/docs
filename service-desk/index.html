# Service desk orders

This is a pilot page. We aim to list predefined service orders that may speed up your delivery time.

TIP

Click the blue buttons on the pages below to get going with your specific order.

  • Lab user orders. This page list service orders that can be requested by lab users, such as system access and lab access.
  • Lab orders. This page list service orders that can be requested by lab leaders and lab coordinators, such as adding new lab users, changing storage and compute resources and data transfers.
  • Data space orders. This page list service orders that can be requested by data space leaders and data space compliance coordinators, such as adding new labs and external data exports.
  • Service center orders. This page list service orders that can be requested by service center leaders and service center administrative coordinators, such as adding new data spaces.
