# Data space mangement

# New data space

Click the button below to order a new data space under your existing service center.

Request a new data space

  • Who can order: Service center leaders and administrative contacts for service centers.
  • Required attachment: (1) A signed data space order, and (2) an invoice profile for the data space.
  • Expected response time: One week.
  • Cost: There is no direct cost for the service center. Data space subscription cost and attached resources in labs are billed as specified in the attached invoice profile in accordance with the Services specifications and Price list.
