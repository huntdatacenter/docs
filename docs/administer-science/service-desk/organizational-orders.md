# Service desk orders for organizations

**This page list predefined service orders for organizations. To place an order, click the blue button and send us a pre-filled email.**

[[toc]]

::: tip Save time

It saves you time to send us the request from your **organizational email**. We use this for identification. Requests from private emails need additional verification that takes longer.

:::

Send us a regular [email](/contact) with your request if you can't find what you are looking for on the this page.


## Organizational access

### Request organizational access to HUNT Cloud

Click on the blue button below to request HUNT Cloud access for your organization. 

Organizational access requires an active Services agreement and Data processor agreement between your organization and us. We will use the information you provide in the attachment to generate the agreements and send them to you for signature. 

We are happy to guide you and answer any questions during this processes, so don't hesitate to [contact us](/contact) if you need assistance.

<SDButton form="request_organizational_access" />

* **Who can order:** Representatives for the organizational unit that request access.
* **Required attachment:** [A completed organizational access form.](/administer-science/agreements/downloads#organizational-access-request-form)
* **Expected response time:** Days.
* **Expected delivery time:** One week.
* **Expected delivery:** Data processor agreement and Services agreement returned on email ready to be signed.
* **Cost:** None.





## Tingweek


### Update Lagtun representative

Lagtun is the body that appoints time to govern the scientific developments of HUNT Cloud. Lagtun members may appoint representatives that receive invitations and meeting information on behalf of their organization.

<SDButton form="request_update_lagtun_rep" />

* **Who can order:** Data controller administrators, Service center administrators, Data space leaders and compliance administrators on the BLUE subscription level.
* **Required attachment:** None.
* **Expected delivery time:** Days.
* **Cost:** None.

### Deregister to future Tingweek invitations

All active HUNT Cloud users will be invited to individual Tings three times per year during our Tingweeks. Participation is voluntary and you can deregister to receive future invitations here.

<SDButton form="deregister_tingweek_invitations" />

* **Who can order:** All data controller administrators.
* **Expected delivery time:** Days.
* **Expected response time:** Confirmation of deregistration.
* **Cost:** Included in your data space subscription.
