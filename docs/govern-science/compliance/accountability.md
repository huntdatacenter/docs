# Accountability

**Accountability for HUNT Cloud's compliance activities are as follows:** 

HUNT Cloud is owned by the [Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU)](https://www.ntnu.edu), Trondheim, Norway. Enterprise number 974 767 880.

The Dean of the Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences, [Siri Forsmo](https://www.ntnu.edu/mh/about/management-group) is responsible for our overall activities.

HUNT Cloud is organized under the Research section at the Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences that is led by [Solrun Johanne Valen](https://www.ntnu.edu/employees/solrun.j.valen).

The Head of HUNT Cloud, [Oddgeir Lingaas Holmen](https://www.ntnu.edu/employees/oddgeir.lingaas.holmen), is responsible for HUNT Cloud's day-to-day operations, including management of information security and privacy, and the overall scientific development of products and services.

The Chief Technology Officer at HUNT Cloud, [Matus Kosut](https://www.ntnu.no/ansatte/matus.kosut), is responsible for the technical development and operations at HUNT Cloud.

The Chief Compliance Officer at HUNT Cloud, [Qussay Ghazeia](https://www.ntnu.edu/employees/qussay.ghazeia), is responsible for the implementation our internal and external audit programs.

Our official affiliation is HUNT Cloud, Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences, Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU), Trondheim, Norway.
