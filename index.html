# Welcome to the HUNT Cloud Documentation

HUNT Cloud is a scientific computing environment located at NTNU (opens new window) in Norway.

These pages contains official user documentation on our services. The content is aimed at lab users and data space coordinators.

Read our eBook (opens new window) and visit our HUNT Cloud (opens new window) pages for general information on our services, and see our Ting reports for detailed updates on our activities.
