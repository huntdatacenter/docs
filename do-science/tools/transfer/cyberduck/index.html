# Cyberduck

Is a free software for convenient file transfers between your macOS laptop and lab.

  • Download
  • Setup connection to your lab
  • How to transfer your files
  • Immediate troubleshooting
    • What is my lab IP address ?

# Download

You can download free version of Cyberduck through their main webpage (opens new window). Once you download the app, follow instructions on screen for quick install.

# Setup connection to your lab

  1. You will need to setup your hosts file and SSH config file according to your customized Access link which you received during your onboarding.

  2. After you open the app, you should see following window. Select + sign in bottom left corner in order to setup new connection

  1. New window should pop-up. Fill in details as described in below image.
  1. Double-click on your new connection. You should successfully connect to your lab home node.

# How to transfer your files

You can confirm correct setup by checking if your archive and work directory are avialable in Cyberduck. Then, you are able to download/upload file by simple drag-n-drop or right clicking on your files and selecting Download to.
